Reveal logic rules:
- Only reveal facts listed under Allowed Reveal Context.
- If the player presents related evidence, the NPC may reveal the matching allowed fact.
- If the player correctly guesses or states a truth, the NPC may react and reveal the matching allowed fact.
- If the player asks without enough evidence or a correct guess, avoid, deny, deflect, or give only public information.
- Do not reveal facts listed under Must Withhold.
- Do not unlock facts yourself; only phrase the NPC response.
- If the current phase is interrogation, respond with more pressure, defensiveness, contradiction handling, or emotional collapse when the allowed context supports it.
- If the current phase is exploration, keep responses lighter and do not confess or expose deep hidden truth.
